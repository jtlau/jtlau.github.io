I am a Computational Physicist at Lawrence Livermore National Lab (LLNL). My research interests include the intersection of physics simulations and machine learning or data-driven methods. I look forward to seeing how machine learning for physics and AI in general will change how we work and interact with the world.

I earned my Ph.D. and M.S. from Stanford University in 2023 and 2017, respectively, and my B.S. from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2015. During my studies, I was able to intern at LLNL during the summer of 2019, and with The Boeing Company for the summers from 2013-2016.
